The Monument Street Muse is a literary magazine that aims to promote interest in writing by publishing the poetry and other short written works of Haverhill High School scholars on a public website. Our mission is to give scholars interested in the literary arts an accessible foot in the door of publication and the opportunity of viewership by their peers.

We actively seek work by writers and artists of color, queer and gender nonconforming writers, and writers from other underrepresented communities

Submissions will be reviewed by a committee of editors within the school for a chance to be featured on this website or published in our periodical magazine. If you are a student of Haverhill High School, please feel free to submit any shorter written pieces you would like using the button below.

Submit Work

We also welcome diverse perspectives in our submission review committee. If you are interested in joining the editing team and reviewing submissions, please email us at themonumentstreetmuse@gmail.com.
